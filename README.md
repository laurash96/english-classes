# English Classes

Interactive teaching materials for adult English learners — designed and taught by **Laura Saldarriaga**.

Built with [Dogme ELT](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Dogme_language_teaching) and [Task-Based Learning](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Task-based_language_teaching) principles. No textbooks. No grammar tables. Just real conversations from day one.

---

## Levels

### 🌱 A1 → A2 — First Steps
Four classes covering the essentials for absolute beginners:

| Class | Topic | Grammar focus |
|-------|-------|---------------|
| 1 | Nice to Meet You | Verb To Be · personal identity |
| 2 | My People | He / She / Have · describing family & friends |
| 3 | A Typical Day | Present Simple · routines & habits |
| 4 | Do You…? | Yes/No questions · short answers |

### 🔥 A2 → B1 — Back to Basics
Four classes for intermediate learners ready to sound more natural:

| Class | Topic | Grammar focus |
|-------|-------|---------------|
| 1 | Who Are You at Parties? | Verb To Be · personal identity beyond job titles |
| 2 | Sundays & Other Lies | Present Simple · real routines & family |
| 3 | Spill the Tea | Describing people · gossip as grammar |
| 4 | Caught in the Act | Present Continuous · what's happening right now |

## Methodology

- **Dogme ELT** — conversation-driven, materials-light, student-generated content
- **Task-Based Learning (TBL)** — students complete real tasks before grammar is explained
- **Andragogy** — designed specifically for adult learners with relevant, non-childish contexts
- **WhatsApp missions** — short voice notes between classes to maintain momentum
- **L1 scaffolding** — strategic Spanish support in student materials at A1–A2 level
