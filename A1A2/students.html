Common errors — Class 1
✗
I am have 28 years.
✓
I'm 28. / I'm 28 years old.

Age uses "be" in English — not "have". I'm 28. Simple.

✗
I am student.
✓
I'm a student.

Always "a" before a job or role. In Spanish there's no article — in English there is.

Is or Has?
✗
She have brown eyes.
✓
She has brown eyes.

Physical features → always "has" (not "have") with she/he.

✗
My sister is a brown hair.
✓
My sister has brown hair.

Physical description → "has". Personality → "is". Simple rule.

Common errors — Class 3
✗
I no drink coffee.
✓
I don't drink coffee.

"No" alone doesn't negate a verb in English. Always "don't" (I/you/we/they) or "doesn't" (he/she).

✗
She wakes up and drink coffee.
✓
She wakes up and drinks coffee.

The -s lives on every verb in the sentence with she/he — not just the first one.

Your mission today~26 min

The Magazine Interview: You are a celebrity being interviewed for a magazine. Your classmate is the journalist. Then you swap.

1
Pick a celebrity or a future version of yourself. Write 3 facts about her in the doc.
2
The journalist asks at least 5 questions using the frames. Celebrity answers with short answers.
3
Swap! Now you're the journalist. Use different questions.
Short answers you need today: "Do you like dogs?" → "Yes, I do!" / "No, I don't." "Are you married?" → "Yes, I am." / "No, I'm not."
