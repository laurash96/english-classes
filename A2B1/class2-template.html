Welcome to My Life
A foreign friend is visiting you for the first time. She doesn't know your family, your city, or your weekend rituals. Write her a guide — in English, in Present Simple.
⏱ Writing time: 10 minutes
🎯 Goal: Present Simple — affirmative, negative, questions
✅ Authentic > perfect
1
Each person writes her own section. Don't look at what the other is writing — you'll read it aloud later.
2
Use Present Simple. Write at least 6 sentences. Authentic wins over perfect every time.
3
If you get stuck on a word, write it in Spanish and keep going. The teacher will sort it out together with you.
Section 1 — Family Life
Student A
Who lives with you? Who is part of your closest circle? Describe them briefly.
Who calls / texts you the most? How often? What do they usually say?
What does your mom / a family member think you should do differently?
Is there something your family does every week / month without fail?

Section 2 — Weekend Life
Student B
What do you usually do on Saturdays? And Sundays?
What do you eat, watch, or listen to when you relax?
What do you always intend to do on weekends but never actually do?
Is there a ritual, a place, or a person that defines your weekend?
